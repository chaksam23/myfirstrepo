Princial's Desk

No institution comes from the blue. And every remarkable incident in the history of mankind has its own prelude. On the auspicious day of 3rd February in the year of 1957, the residents of the locality of nabagram happen to witness such an epoch making event to Cater the burgeoning demands for dissemination of Higher Education among the local populace- Nabagram Hiralal Paul College was founded. But many a real hero of social reformation of and remain unsung. Quite appropriately goes the saying in the locality of Nabagram that Nabagram could not dream of a college without Hiralal Paul. Duda indefatigable agility, philanthropic and altruistic endeavours of late Hiralal Paul the college was established. Dr BC Roy the then chief Minister government of West Bengal laid the foundation stone. Hiralal Babu had a dream to realise," I have just been able to plant a little sapling for the dissemination of Higher Education and it is impregnated with the potentiality of growing into a big tree with full Blossom if nourished with care in the years to come."
